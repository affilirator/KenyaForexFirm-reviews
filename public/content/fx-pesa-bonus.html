1. Deposit Bonus

This is the most common type. FXPesa offers a percentage of your deposit as trading credit, typically around 30%. For instance, deposit $200, and you might get a $60 bonus, starting you with $260 in trading equity.

Pro: Instantly boosts your trading capital.

Con: Comes with strict trading volume requirements before withdrawal.

2. No-Deposit Bonus

Less common but highly sought after. FXPesa occasionally offers these to new clients, providing a small amount of starting capital (e.g., $30) just for registering an account. It's a risk-free way to test their platform.

Pro: Trade live markets without risking your own money.

Con: Profit withdrawal is heavily restricted and often capped.

3. Loyalty & Rebate Programs

These reward consistent trading activity. Instead of an upfront bonus, you receive cash back based on the volume you trade. For example, you might get $2 back for every standard lot traded, paid out monthly.

Pro: Rewards your actual trading activity with withdrawable cash.

Con: Benefits only become significant for high-volume traders.

The Verdict: Bonus Value vs. Trading Volume

Not all bonuses are created equal. The true value lies in the relationship between the bonus amount and the difficulty of meeting the volume requirements. Bonuses with excessively high volume targets can encourage over-trading and are often not worth pursuing.
